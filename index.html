How to Play Simon Says
  • Start the Game: Press any key on your keyboard to begin.
  • Watch Carefully: One of the colored buttons will flash.
  • Repeat the Sequence: Click the button(s) in the exact same order.
  • Level Up: Each new level adds one more step to the sequence.
  • Keep Going: Try to remember the whole pattern as it gets longer.
  • Game Over: If you click the wrong button, the game ends and your score is shown.
  • Restart: Press any key to play again.
